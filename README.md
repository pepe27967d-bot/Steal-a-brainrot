# 🧠 Steal a Brainrot - Web Game

A fun multiplayer-inspired web game where you collect brainrots, earn passive income, steal from enemies, and defend your base!

## 🎮 How to Play

### Core Mechanics

1. **🏪 Buy Brainrots**
   - Click on any brainrot in the shop to buy it
   - Each brainrot generates passive income per second
   - Higher rarity = higher price + more income

2. **💰 Earn Passive Income**
   - Your brainrots automatically generate money every second
   - Use the income to buy more brainrots
   - Higher multiplier = more income!

3. **👾 Steal from Enemies**
   - Click on an enemy's unlocked base to steal a brainrot
   - You'll be holding the stolen brainrot
   - **Important:** You can only carry one at a time!

4. **🏠 Deposit at Your Base**
   - Once you steal a brainrot, return to your base
   - Click the "Deposit at Base" button to secure your loot
   - Your base must be unlocked for you to deposit

5. **🔒 Lock Your Base**
   - Click "Lock Base" to protect your brainrots for 15 seconds
   - Enemies cannot steal from a locked base
   - Use this when you're being attacked!

6. **⚔️ Combat System**
   - Click "Equip Bat" to get ready for combat
   - With a bat equipped, click enemies to attack them
   - Attacking slows down enemy thieves

7. **🔄 Rebirth**
   - Click "Rebirth" to reset your brainrots
   - You'll get a x2 multiplier for future runs
   - Great for scaling up your income!

## 🎮 Controls

- **Buy Brainrots**: Click on shop items
- **Steal**: Click on enemy bases
- **Lock Base**: Click "Lock Base" button (or press L)
- **Equip Bat**: Click "Equip Bat" button (or press B)
- **Deposit**: Click "Deposit at Base" when holding a brainrot
- **Rebirth**: Click "Rebirth" button

## 💡 Tips & Strategies

1. **Early Game**: Buy cheap brainrots to build your income
2. **Mid Game**: Save up for epic/legendary brainrots
3. **Late Game**: Focus on rebirthing for multipliers
4. **Stealing**: Wait for enemies to unlock their bases
5. **Defense**: Lock your base when enemies are attacking
6. **Income**: More expensive brainrots = exponentially better income
